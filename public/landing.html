Common questions

Is this just ChatGPT with a prompt?

No. Sessionly is built on a structured session schema — every output has a warm-up, core, reflection, debrief, timing, materials, and safety. You get a plan you can run, not a paragraph you have to rework.

Can I edit what the AI generates?

Yes. Every block is inline-editable. You can also regenerate a single block (just the warm-up, just the reflection) instead of starting over.

Can I use it for in-person and online sessions?

Yes. Tell the generator whether the session is indoor, outdoor, online, or hybrid — it adapts materials, instructions, and safety notes accordingly.

Do you support andragogy, geragogy, and movement-based learning?

Yes. Sessionly generates sessions for adults, older learners, and movement-focused groups — with age-appropriate warm-ups, pacing, and safety.

Do you offer refunds?

Pro is month-to-month — cancel any time from your account. Annual plans have a 14-day refund window, no questions asked.
