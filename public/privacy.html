Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-18

1. What we collect

  • Account data: email address, authentication identifier.
  • Session inputs: the briefs you submit (group size, goal, duration, notes).
  • Generated outputs: the session plans we produce for you.
  • Usage data: quota counters, timestamps, plan state.
  • Billing data: handled by Stripe. We store only the subscription status, not card details.

2. How we use it

To operate the service, enforce quotas, fulfil paid subscriptions, respond to support requests, and improve the product. We do not sell your data.

3. AI processing

To generate session plans, your inputs are sent to an AI provider (e.g. OpenAI). We do not use your inputs to train public models. Do not submit personal data you would not want processed by a third-party AI provider.

4. Storage and retention

Account and session data is stored on managed infrastructure (Supabase, Vercel). You can request deletion at any time by emailing hello@sessionly.app.

5. Your rights (GDPR / UK GDPR / equivalents)

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your data, and to object to processing. Contact hello@sessionly.app to exercise these rights.

6. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies for authentication and session state. We do not use advertising cookies.

7. Subprocessors

  • Vercel — hosting
  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • OpenAI — AI generation
  • Stripe — payment processing

8. Contact

Questions? hello@sessionly.app.
